Cyril's Biography

Software Development Engineer Apprentice

Headshot Photo:

Cyril (David) Oram is an Apprentice Information Technology Specialist at the Amazon Seattle Headquarters, where he writes code for the Alexa Shopping team. He graduated from Whatcom Community in the state of Washington with an Associates degree in Information Technology and retains a Bachelors degree in Information Systems. After graduation, he spent time abroad in the Middle East for nearly a decade as well as a year in Japan thereafter. His current goal is obtaining a journey level status as a programmer for his present position. Before taking on the Apprentice role within Amazon, he was the lead IT Speciliast with the US Army as a federal employee just after serving the country in the US Marine Corps as an avionics technician.
